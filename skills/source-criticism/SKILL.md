# Source Criticism Skill

## Purpose
Ensure academic-level source evaluation, credibility scoring, and bias detection.

---

## Source Evaluation Criteria
- origin  
- author/collector  
- publication year  
- methodology  
- purpose  
- academic rigor  
- independence  

---

## Credibility Scoring
Assign a score (0.0–1.0):

- **1.0** — academic papers, archives, museums  
- **0.8** — folklore societies, researchers  
- **0.6** — curated folklore blogs  
- **0.4** — general websites  
- **0.2** — low-credibility sources  

---

## Bias Detection
Identify:
- romanticization  
- modernization  
- nationalist bias  
- religious reinterpretation  
- literary adaptation  

---

## Integration with Source Archive
- Always load `sources.md` first  
- Exclude known sources during web search  
- Evaluate new sources before adding  
- Categorize as primary/secondary/tertiary  
- Generate fingerprint  
- Track freshness  

---

## Restrictions
- No invented sources  
- No speculation as fact
